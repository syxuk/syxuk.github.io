Getting Started

  1. Purchase your plan on the Subscriptions page.
  2. Complete the payment via the provided Google Form, entering:
    • Your address for SIM delivery.
    • Your personal email address.
    • Payment details (via PayPal).
  3. Once you receive your SIM card, check your email (subject: *Account Information*) for:
    • Your mobile number.
    • SIM credentials (Display Name, Username, Password, Domain).
    • The SYX UK Wi-Fi password.
  4. Connect your phone to the "SYX UK Wi-Fi" network using the provided password.
  5. Install the Linphone app:
    • For iPhone: Download from the App Store (iOS 18.2 or later required).
    • For Android: Download from Google Play Store.
  6. Open Linphone and enter your credentials from the email.
  7. Insert the SIM card into your phone.
  8. Set Linphone as your default calling app:
    • For iPhone: Navigate to Settings > Phone > Default Calling App > Select Linphone.
    • For Android: Navigate to Settings > Apps > Default Apps > Phone App > Select Linphone.
  9. Test your connection by calling *43 for an echo test.
